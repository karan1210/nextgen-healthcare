Ornidazole 1% + Chlorhexidine

Our advanced antiseptic and antibacterial gel delivers superior efficacy in managing oral infections. This innovative formulation combines antimicrobial agents to provide comprehensive treatment for various dental conditions.

Clinical Applications: Gingivitis Management, Periodontitis Treatment, Oral Ulcer Therapy, Post-Surgical Dental Care

AUGMEXIN-M Forte Gel

Proprietary Composition
Chlorhexidine Gluconate + Metronidazole + Lignocaine Hydrochloride

A breakthrough triple-action dental gel combining powerful antiseptic, antimicrobial, and analgesic properties. This innovative formulation provides comprehensive oral care through synergistic therapeutic action.

Therapeutic Benefits: Rapid Pain Relief, Enhanced Infection Control, Accelerated Tissue Healing

S-OFF Therapeutic Oral Rinse

Proprietary Composition
Potassium Nitrate 3% + Sodium Fluoride 0.2% + Triclosan 0.3%

An advanced therapeutic rinse specifically formulated for dental hypersensitivity management. Our proprietary blend provides immediate relief while offering long-term protection and enamel strengthening.

Key Features: Desensitizing Action, Enamel Fortification, Antibacterial Protection, Long-lasting Relief

MESACLIN Forte - Gynecological Innovation

Proprietary Composition
Clindamycin 100mg + Clotrimazole 200mg + Tinidazole 100mg

A sophisticated triple-action vaginal capsule delivering comprehensive treatment for complex gynecological infections. This innovative formulation provides broad-spectrum coverage in a single, convenient dosage form.

Therapeutic Spectrum: Antibacterial, Antifungal, and Antiprotozoal Activity for Comprehensive Infection Management

AUGMEXIN-P Surgical Ointment

Proprietary Composition
Ornidazole 1% + Povidone Iodine 5%

An advanced surgical wound care ointment combining broad-spectrum antimicrobial action with superior healing properties. Our dual-action formulation provides comprehensive post-operative care.

Clinical Applications: Post-Operative Wound Care, Infection Prevention, Enhanced Tissue Repair, Surgical Site Management

HY-KUL Multi-Action Cream

Proprietary Composition
Hydrocortisone + Lignocaine + Phenylephrine + Calamine

An innovative four-component formulation for comprehensive anorectal care. This sophisticated blend combines anti-inflammatory, analgesic, vasoconstrictor, and soothing properties for optimal patient comfort and therapeutic efficacy.

Clinical Indications: Hemorrhoids, Anal Fissures, Pruritus Ani, Post-Surgical Anorectal Care
